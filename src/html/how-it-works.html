How Does The Programme Work?

The programme works in 4 steps

Physician Approval

Our programmes are tailored to patients with obesity. We have partnered with the best weight-loss physicians, Zur Rose/Telemedicine Partner, who will review your medical history, and ensure that you should be on this.

Delivery

If the 3rd party physician approves, you will start the programme and the correct weight-loss prescription medication will be sent to your home via our pharmacy delivery partner Zur Rose/e-Pharmacy.

Goal-Setting and Tracking

You will get a coach assigned to you via text message and app, who will track your weight-loss and make sure they align with the goals you’ve set.

Results and Refill

You will get a new medication delivery every month until your prescription runs out. If you are seeing results and would like to continue, you can contact your physician via the app to see if you quality for a prolonged prescription.

What do I get other than medication?

Lab Grade Measurements

BMI is not a perfect measurement and neither is weight alone in isolation. We use cutting edge technology to track not only your weight, but also your body measurements to ensure that you are seeing your progress on yourself and not just a scale.

Stress Management

Mental health is important to maintain a consistent weight-loss routine. You will get access to a variety of stress management programs ranging from meditation and yoga to daily planning. These tools will be available on your smartphone so you can do them in the comfort of your own home.

Healthy Eating Recipes

Our nutrionists work with the latest science to create delicious diets that are both delicious and low cost. Via your personal coach they can tailor these recipes to your dietary restrictions or preferences. You can also browse through any community created recipes that have been vetted by our nutrionists as supporting weight-loss.

Coaching

All our programmes come with personal coaching. A coach ensures that you stay on the programme, and that you consistently see progress towards your weight-loss goals. If it becomes too much, or you can even handle a little more, you can always bring it up with your coach.

Our partners

Physicians

We focus on remaining impartial to not recommend medication that you do not need. As a result, your prescription as well as medical advice, come from a 3rd party physician. We partner with TeleClinic via Zur Rose who have the most extensive network of physicians, to ensure that you can get your answers in a timely fashion.

You can read more about them here.

Pharmacies

Our partner pharmacies all accept the EU standard ePrescription. Which means that even if you move to another EU country, we will still be able to fulfill your order. We use Zur Rose’s extensive pharmacy network to deliver your medication to your address. They also ensure to transport your medication at the optimical condition so that it lasts longer.

You can read more about them here.
